Our Board

Loch Lomond Villa Board of Directors

Loch Lomond Villa is governed by a 10-member volunteer Board of Directors. The Simonds Lions Club, which continues to be represented on the Board, along with the other Directors, make up the members of the Corporation.

The Board of Directors is legally and morally responsible for the home and the services it provides. The major responsibility of the Board is to ensure that adequate and proper care is being delivered in a quality manner consistent with the needs of those being served.

NBANH

(New Brunswick Association of Nursing Homes)

NBANH represents 65 nursing homes in the province of New Brunswick. The main objectives of the Association are to promote a high standard of care and efficiency in nursing homes, to provide an opportunity for consultation and to take united action on all matters of common interest and concern to the Association. For more information, please visit the NBANH Web Site at: www.nbanh.com
